Frequently Asked Questions

What is hibiscus tea?

Hibiscus tea is made from the dried petals of the hibiscus flower, offering a refreshing, tart, and vibrant drink known for its health benefits.

What are the health benefits of hibiscus tea?

It is rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, can help with blood pressure management, and supports overall heart health.

How do I brew hibiscus tea?

To brew, steep 1-2 teaspoons of dried hibiscus petals in hot water for about 5-7 minutes, then strain and enjoy!

Is hibiscus tea caffeine-free?

Yes, hibiscus tea is naturally caffeine-free, making it a great option for any time of day.
